I like experience life with my own body.

That is to say, there is a difference between doing so and something else. My friend, Gu, if I may say so, is a person who thinks more on life. He is a jail keeper, at the sametime a young aritst, a painter. Not a professional though, but expert enough that you may think he may have attended a proper art school or he is at least going to do so (Everybody who have seen his work would think so). He reckons often on his life, and many others, and then he presents his thoughts with his pencil. Me, on the other hand, I experience, with a rather empty mind. I hold the believe that the more valuable core of life lies not ahead, but in the past. And only by remembering, can a person realise what is what. Naturally, I write down things from time to time, and have always wanted to be a proper writer. But in reality, I’m a staff in a super big company in a super big city, doing daily work that I hardly find a meaning in it. But as I said, I like to experience life with my body. Therefore it’s still too early for me to make judgements.
