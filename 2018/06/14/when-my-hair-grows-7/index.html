A few days later, tatoo girl asked me out to go buy a guitar.
“Nice. I was always thinking to learn an instrument myself.”
“But what would you want to buy?”
“Guitar is alright. It’s what everybody plays at our age. Besides, people say it’s the sexest.”
“I’m so happy to hear this. We could make a band together. Two guitar is perfect to do so.”
“Oh I really wish.” I was getting excited with this picture. But since I know nothing about guitar, there is nothing I can suggest about.
Not that I was only trying to be cool with tatoo girl. Yeah I know she has a tatoo and she was cool. But who said I can’t be cool myself? I mean cool from my own nature. Everybody has a dream to be a musicion, so do I. When I was a child, and was asked to talked about my dream. I really hesitated upon the list of careers that they gave us - Artist, Scientist, Astronut, Musicion, Writter, Mathematicist, bus driver, eletricion and so on. While my classmates were one by one asked to stand up and named their dream, I was getting very nervous with time going by cause I really coudn’t make my choice. So in the end I chose all of them. I wrote it down every name that I could think out and then when it was my turn, I annouced proudly from my paper, “When I grow up, I want to be the following…”. The whole class laughed at me awhile me reading my list. But that’s not the point, the point is being musicion was on my list.

Tatoo girl had did some research, she knew a place where we could get really cheap guitar with normal quality. It’s going to be so cheap that we won’t feel guilty leaving it with dust if realizing we don’t like the idea. “Remember, it’s two hours away, in the north of Beijing, very far away. There is the place where they sells everything. And that’s where we are going.”
I have no other suggestions so we simply got ready after returning from library and got going. Normally it would be efficient to take the tube. But that day the weather was good with clear air so we decided to take the bus.

Along the road, there was people making long queue in font of shops which sell chestnut. It’s the chestnut season then and the shops simply roasted it in a chestnut machine with sugar and it came out hot and soft and tasty. Girls like them. They always know where to get good things and enjoy. Whereas boys would only wanted it when someone presented it in front of their nose. Boys were always less capable of living well those days.

We were almost hungry as soon as we got onto the bus, but we had better thing to resist the hunger. “I obviously can’t sing. So I will be the guitar with music only. “I said.
“Pity. I can do the singing and the playing though.” Tatoo girl said.
“What about composer? Who is going to be that for us. We can’t always play others song, can we.”
“Well that’s indeed a problem. We can give an interview to hire someone if we need. There must be people good at it.”
“Right.”
Not soon after we got on the bus, we got two spare seats so we took it. It was much more comfortable than standing. We had less the pressure to talk to each. She was toward the window and looked out, me torward her facing the window. We were no longer talking about ourselves anymore since we were not facing each other. Instead we started to enjoy the beautiful view of outside of the window. The shops, the people in their thin automn coat, the streets, pale blue sky in the west. It must have been the excitment of thinking about guitar that exhausted us so we soon fell asleep deeply. By the time the tatoo girl woke up, there was almost nobody on the bus, only people getting on with their shopping bags then left in the next stop. The view out side of the window now was less shinny but covered with more shade and seemed dusty. It was as if tatoo girl could sense it that I’ve awakened a few minutes earlier that she soon woke up her self and was startled.
“Are wer sitll alrihgt?” I asked her while she chekcing her map on her phone.
“Thankfully yes.” Then she dropped her wrist and fell asleep again.
