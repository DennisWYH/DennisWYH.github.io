  |   873 parole   |   5 mins Posted on 2018-06-19 | In novel , when my hair grows

The bus finally arrived. We got off the bus with our body sore. The last part of the trip was not on a smoothly paved way. When I stood on my own foot, I felt the ground still shaking. Views around dusty and grey, leaves on the tree covered with very old dust, only waiting for a rain to wash it away. Rains will come soon - the automn rains, but none knows when and how. Only the fact that when it would come, the temperature would drop, and on every occation of the rain, the temperature drop more, until it doesn’t rain anymore. The snow comes and taked it’s place. For the leaves on the tree, there is no way to save them, shortly after the rain, they would be back like how they were again. This is the true color of Beijing. The color of dust, and dust can be of many colors.

I was delighted to see many things. They are all new to me. Walking along with tatoo girl, who held her phone checking the map on it, I kept looking around like an busy bee. There was the factory area ahead of us. Dull metal layered factory house, long in shape and dusty just like the rest. We walked toward it, through a channel in between two seperated houses, we got into the business world we were looking for. In between the two rows of the buildings there were small shops with glass door on their front. In side and out side of the door, there were the products, one upon another. It was weekend that day, there were buyers walking in the main path, most of who harvested something in the plastic bags they were carrying. You can really find everything there, toiletware, kitchen ware, lights for house, bedding stuff…etc. It’s basically a walmart located in a rural area with one third of the price. We knew we were at the right place now, so now it’s only a matter of finding the right part of it. Tatoo girl could finally put away her phone and walked with me full heart. She started to enjoy the shabbiness and the livelyness like I did. It was amazing how huge the enviroment can differ shortly after two hours bus trip. The sky extremly clear. The sun slanting but still gave it’s heat.

We walked almost every corner of the area, until the omen that there was the chance guitars don’t exist there starting to bother us, and we lost our fun spirit to explore. So we started to ask around, and that was what make it even more desperate. The answer was alwasy no. “I don’t know there is guitars around, do you know it?” The shop owner answered with his eye brows frowned, and turned to his parter. “What is a guitar?” The partner answered. We thanked and left before the explanation of what a guitar is happened. I was not complaining at all. I was tired, sweating a lot, foot starting to hurt. But I was not complaining. Tatoo girl was getting a more serious face, blaming herself for the unfruitfull trip. I kept telling her that it was alright, that it must be somewhere around here that we missed. But the encourage must have gone wrong, cause there was simply no sight of anything about instruments. So we stopped talking but only searching with fast pace.She was walking faster and faster, and I coudn’t kept up with her with my sore foot. The distance between us began to expand, until I have to stop her, “Hey, why don’t we go looking for it seperatly.” She nodded on it and dispeared. I realized that I was slightly out of temper myself. My tone must not sounded nice. But I was indeed very tired, I stopped at a food shop and got myself an ice cream. Soon the weather would be too cold for an ice cream and all you want would just to get inside of the buildings. You regret for not wearing thick enough sweathers and the tempertarure alwasy goes one step ahead of you so you end up feeling cold all the time. Until winter truly comes. Your sweater and the winter wind would find their balance. And you would survive the winter of the city. That was what I thought about when I had that ice cream. The ice cream only made me more thirsty but I would keep it with me at the moment. I started walking on my foot again and this time I felt alright. It’s not just about two rows of shops here, behind of them, there was another row connected with a channel and another one behind that. The channels opened at different places as if they were designed ramdom. I went back and forth a few times just to search for the channels because they may be stuffed with goods making it unrecognizable. I soon realized it was such a maze we were dealing with and we should really made our strategy first. The sunlight now started to slant even more that it glowed with pure goldness.
